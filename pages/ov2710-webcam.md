## Assembly
Required parts:
* OV2710 USB camera module, 38mm square
* 2x M2x8mm socket head cap screw
* 2x M3x12mm socket head cap screw
* 1x M2x16mm socket head cap screw
* 5x M2 hex nut
* 1x M5x20mm button head cap screw
* 1x M5 hex nut
* 1x 1/4-20 hex nut (optional)
